Shining talent are in dark

Sportohunt ,a startup ,simply with profound vision of being support hand for those who have burning dreams of sports career ,but due to lack of a platform where talent can showcase their talent ,lack of a proper coaching and support etc, their shining talent are in dark. Sportohunt is here for you to let your talent shine with full potential.

The problems faced to make career in sports

Platform

Lack of platform to showcase talent.

Coaching

Lack of proper coaching .

Sponsorship

There is no sponsorship or support.

Diet plan and discipline

Sports enthusiasts do not know the proper diet plan and discipline to follow.

Recognition of talent

There is no recognition of talents on the level they deserve.

How We Work

Our work process

How SPORTOHUNT is going to solve these problems:-

Provide Platform

Sportohunt is going to provide a platform to the talents from where they will be able to showcase their talent to large audience.

Provide support

All those who will show progress by seeing tutorials and will give great performance in the events organised by SPORTOHUNT will be given sponsorship and full support to perform at state and national level.

Video tutorials

Sportohunt will have video tutorials on sports and games which will provide excellent coaching to those who are wishing to make mark on this world with their talent. e.g., if you have desire to learn chess and want to become pro at it then you will be able to do so by learning from video tutorial.

Top performers

All top performers categorised based on performance in events organised by SPORTOHUNT will be directly projected on front page of website which will help them to get sponsorship and recognition on the level they will deserve.

Guidance

We will be uploading video tutorials on every top most played sports and games in INDIA and according to fitness required forspecific sport , we will be providing them guidance from top most fitness expert.
